THE PIT

PumpPit is a pixel-art survival arcade game on Solana. Connect your Phantom wallet, enter the dungeon, and survive relentless enemy waves to earn real $PIT tokens.

Each wallet gets exactly one run. No second chances. No replays. Your score is permanent — make it count.

// how it works

HOW TO PLAY

🔮
CONNECT

Connect your Phantom wallet. This is your one and only run — no wallet, no entry.

🎮
SURVIVE

WASD or Arrow Keys. Enemies deal damage on contact. Reach 0 HP and it's over — forever.

⚡
COLLECT

Grab power-ups, open chests, and level up to stand a chance against the waves.

💰
CLAIM

Sign your score with Phantom. $PIT tokens are distributed when the contract deploys.

// arsenal

WEAPONS & UPGRADES

— WEAPONS —

PISTOL

High-speed single shots. Your starting weapon. 3 upgrade tiers.

MACHINE GUN

9 rounds per second. Melts hordes. Unlocks spread shot upgrade.

SHOTGUN

5-pellet burst. Devastating at close range. Upgrades to 7 pellets.

LASER

Instant hit beam. No travel time. Pierces multiple enemies.

GRENADE

Explosive AOE. Massive blast radius. Clears tight clusters instantly.

MISSILES

Homing projectiles. Lock onto enemies mid-flight. Upgrades to 3 at once.

FREEZE

Radial ice burst. Freezes all enemies nearby. Shatter upgrade one-shots frozen foes.

FLAMETHROWER

Continuous fire cone. Burn DOT. 16 hits per second at max rate.

— PASSIVES —

SHIELD

Absorb one hit per charge. Regens over time. Stack up to 3.

ARMOR

Flat damage reduction on every hit. Up to 50% DR at max level.

MAGNET

Pulls XP orbs from farther away at higher speed. Never miss XP again.

SPEED

Raw movement boost. Up to 2× at max. Kite enemies effortlessly.

HEALTH

Raise your max HP pool. Stack it twice for +150 HP total.

CRIT

Chance to deal 3× damage. Up to 60% crit rate at max tier.

DOMAIN EXPANSION

Press SPACE to unleash a shockwave that blasts every enemy outward. 20s cooldown. Zero survivors in range.
